A

The obtained parameters.

B

Fitness statistics (only in case of inspyred 
algorithms: Classical EO, SA)

C

Fitness components: name of fitness function; 
fitness value, calculated by the function; weight 
assigned to the function; the weighted fitness 
value;
the resulting cumulated fitness value.

D

Displays the “state” of the population during the 
evolution. (only for inspyred algorithms)

E

Displays the alleles (only for inspyred 
algorithms ), only useful for single parameter 
tasks.

F

Displays the given proximity of the optimum.
